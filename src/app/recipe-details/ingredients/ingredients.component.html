Ingredients

  • Mixed greens (spinach, arugula, or romaine)
  • Cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • Cucumbers (sliced)
  • Red bell pepper (diced)
  • Carrots (shredded)
  • Purple cabbage (thinly sliced)
  • Avocado (diced)
  • Red onion (thinly sliced)
  • Chickpeas (cooked or canned, rinsed)
  • Sunflower seeds (toasted)
  • Pomegranate seeds
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)

Dressing (Optional):

Olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper to taste.
